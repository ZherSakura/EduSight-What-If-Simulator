# EduSight-What-If-Simulator
## Overview
The EduSight What-If Simulator is an AI-powered decision support tool designed to help educators evaluate the potential impact of intervention strategies on students who are at risk of dropping out. Instead of relying on reactive measures, the simulator enables schools to test different intervention scenarios and predict how a student's risk level may change over time.

By combining historical attendance records, academic performance, and socioeconomic indicators, the simulator provides data-driven forecasts that support proactive and evidence-based decision-making.
